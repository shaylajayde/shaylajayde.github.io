Here are some tips to help you stay safer in the future

Tip: #1

Stop, Look, Think

Did anything look out of the ordinary? Did you recognize the senders address? Was it similar but not the same as an offical email?

Tip: #2

Do you spot a red flag?

Examples of red flags include: Sense of urgency, suspicious links or attachments, unusual requests, spelling errors and more!

Tip: #3

When in doubt throw it out

If you ever think that an email is suspicous it is better to err on the side of caution.
